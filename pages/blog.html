Healthy and Fit After 40

Many people believe that it gets harder to be healthy and fit after 40, and in some ways it truly is. I am not going to sugar coat it but what truly makes it harder for someone over 40 to get fit is their motivation, will and consistency. It may not be your metabolism, genetics or some hex your ex put on you. Unless you have a serious disease or your last partner was a witch or warlock. I won’t mess with the Voodoo that you do.

HEY over 40, let's ride a bike!

At 42, I bought a bicycle hoping to improve my cardio without adding more stress to my knees, ankles, and lower back. What started as a simple fitness choice quickly became one of the most enjoyable parts of my health journey. From the scenic roads near Red Rock Canyon to the growing cycling communities throughout Las Vegas and across the country, cycling offers a fun, low-impact way for people of all ages to improve their health, recovery, and overall quality of life. Discover how one bicycle changed my approach to fitness after 40.
